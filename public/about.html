About LocalShare

Your Local File Sharing Solution

How It Works

LocalShare is designed to make file sharing between devices on the same network simple, fast, and secure. Unlike cloud-based services, LocalShare keeps your files local — no uploads to external servers, no delays. Using WebSocket technology, it connects your devices directly, allowing you to drag, drop, and share files instantly.

Whether you're at home, in the office, or on a shared Wi-Fi, LocalShare ensures your data stays where it belongs: with you.
